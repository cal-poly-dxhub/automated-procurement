Insurance Requirements

1. The partner will maintain workers' compensation, general liability, and construction insurance coverage during the term of the contract.
2. The partner will maintain insurance coverage(s) during the term of the contract for the duration of the project.
3. The partner will provide proof of insurance coverage to the employer upon request.
4. The partner will ensure that any and all subcontractors will be insured according to the requirements outlined in an attached document.
5. The employer is responsible for ensuring that the partner's general liability insurance coverage meets the $1M minimum requirement.
6. The employer reserves the right to request proof of insurance coverage and change the insurance requirements at any time.
